A Quiet and Constant Companion - Music Video

This is a work in progress project; a 2d animated music video worked on solely by myself,
commissioned by the artists, Watson & Lockett, to the song of the same name,
A Quiet and Constant Companion.

As with many of projects, the animation is still firmly in its early iteration, but I thought
it was a good indication of the vision I intend to create, with the overall cartoon artstyle and
pacing reflecting what I aspire to create.

Something I really stuggle with when designing is to take a step back and evaluate how I am going
to create the product I want. Since coming to University I have started to take these vital early
steps into my consideration. This is something I have started to realise after taking design
courses in my first year of my degree, now that I have the skills and techniques to properly plan
out a project in advance, I hope I will return to this animation when I find the time and complete
it to a point where both myself and my clients are happy with it.
